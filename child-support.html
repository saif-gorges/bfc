Hey there, generous mommies and daddies — these babies need child support!

Ever dreamed of contributing to an art project that'll make jaws drop faster than a sandstorm at Black Rock City? Get ready to fuel your wildest dreams, because we're cooking up something spectacular: an art car like no other, complete with a full stage and mind-bending performances.

But here's the kicker — it's not your average art car. It's a roving Baby Fight Club arena.

Picture it: tiny gladiators duking it out on a stage fit for giants, all while surrounded by the surreal beauty of the desert.

Your donations will help us deck out this mobile masterpiece with everything from flame-shooting bassinets to neon-lit rattles. So join us on this wild ride, and let's make a baby brawl to remember. After all — who needs thunder domes when you've got diapers and disco balls?
